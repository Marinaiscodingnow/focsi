  • Brain Break

    • Students can customize Everest's home and outfits. You can obtain different clothes and furniture. The fox, Everest, will congratulate you every time you reach one of your study goals by rewarding you with either clothing or furniture! This ranges with an array of different clothes, including clown shoes, party hats, heels and more. Everest is an all inclusive non specified gender fox who can be anything you want. They only wish to guide you on this journey through all of your studying needs. To assist your studious journey, you can create a timer of your choice, dividing the amount of time you choose to give to a specific assignment or subject, also helping to schedule a time for yourself for a well deserved study break. The break will last around 5 minutes then request you to return back to finish your work. The brain breaks pop up on the screen at random times while on the website and may provide a prompt for you to get food, water, a breathing exercise, or a short brain game. Some may include a wordle, tetris, or a small word search. Students can forget to take the needed time to let their brain rest and regain its maximum functionality often. Therefore with Focsi this problem can be avoided. With Everest and the study breaks we can help you manage your time and get you on top of all of your classes.
